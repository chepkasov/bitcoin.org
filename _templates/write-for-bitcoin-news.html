Write for us

Get compensated for writing news stories on bitcoin.com. We are always looking for talented, passionate writers who can provide high-quality content. We offer payment in bitcoin per written article. Please write your articles in google docs and follow the guidelines below and share it with us at editor@bitcoin.com.

What we are looking for

  • Features
  • Op-Eds
  • Reviews
  • Analysis

Skills and Experience

Knowledge of the English language is a must—mastery is not required, but contributors should be proficient in the language Writing skills: spelling, grammar, and an ability to produce relevant and captivating content Past writing experience is preferred, but not required Contributors must be active members of the Bitcoin community Contributors must have some understanding of Bitcoin, knowledge on how to use Bitcoin, and a passion for digital currency




Article Submission Guidelines

The following guidelines must be adhered to when submitting content to Bitcoin.com. Articles will be chosen for publication based on content and following of the guidelines.

Categories

  • Breaking news
  • Interviews
  • Reviews
  • Technical Write-ups
  • Analysis
  • Op-Ed
  • ect.

Article Format

  • Minimum article length 500 words / Maximum article length 1000 words
  • DO NOT copy paste from other sites. We don’t publish secondary content. Write unique news stories and other unique content with high quality (e.g. exclusive interviews and stories)
  • Make sure your title is descriptive and catchy
  • Keep you title under 156 characters
  • Make sure your article is easy to read. People familiar and not will be reading the articles.
  • Do a proper fact check
  • Write unique content that attracts readers.
  • Make content timely on current subjects.
  • Check your spelling & grammar to make sure it is accurate and reads easily.
  • Do not start your article with a picture. Use first picture after the first paragraph as we use featured images.
  • Make sure to use subheadings H2
  • Leave the proper amount of space between paragraphs.
  • Link to your subjects.
  • List your sources and image sources.
  • BOLD sentences you want to highlight
  • How to write an Op-Ed piece
  • Use bullet points if applicable to give a professional look to the article
  • Always write a disclaimer/disclosure if you write about a company or cryptocurrency that you have interest in.
  • Read through the article twice once you are done to make sure you have it correct.
  • Once we approve and publish your article make sure you promote it on social media (FaceBook, Twitter, Reddit, Linkedin, google plus, etc.)

Notes

Op-Ed articles should have sources and solid reasoning backing up your opinion. Articles should have 1 or more images in them. Think of your readers and what they would want and need to know about your subject. Present articles in a professional manner. Your articles will be seen worldwide.
